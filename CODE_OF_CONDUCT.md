# 🤝 Code of Conduct

**Building a Welcoming and Inclusive Community**

_Our commitment to creating a positive environment for all contributors_

</div>

---

## 🌟 Our Pledge

As contributors and maintainers of the **VideoFetch** project, we are committed to creating a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone. We value the participation of individuals from all backgrounds and perspectives and want to ensure a positive experience for all community members.

**We pledge to make participation in our project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of:**

-   🌍 Age, nationality, or geographic location
-   👥 Race, ethnicity, or cultural background
-   🏳️‍🌈 Sexual orientation or gender identity
-   🧠 Disability status or neurodiversity
-   💼 Professional experience or skill level
-   🎓 Educational background
-   🙏 Religion or personal beliefs
-   📊 Socioeconomic status

---

## ✅ Expected Behavior

We encourage all community members to:

### 🤝 Be Respectful and Considerate

-   Listen to others' opinions, ideas, and contributions with an open mind
-   Acknowledge different perspectives and experiences
-   Treat everyone with courtesy and professionalism

### 🌈 Use Inclusive Language

-   Be mindful of your words and their impact on others
-   Avoid language that excludes or marginalizes any group
-   Choose words that welcome and include all community members

### 💬 Engage in Constructive Communication

-   Provide helpful and constructive feedback
-   Ask questions in good faith and with genuine curiosity
-   Focus on the idea or issue, not the person

### 🤗 Support Fellow Community Members

-   Help newcomers feel welcome and supported
-   Share knowledge and resources generously
-   Celebrate others' contributions and achievements

### 🎯 Focus on What's Best for the Community

-   Prioritize the collective good over personal interests
-   Make decisions that benefit the project and its users
-   Collaborate towards common goals

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable within our community:

### 🚫 Harassment and Discrimination

-   Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
-   Harassment in any form, whether public or private
-   Discriminatory language or behavior
-   Unwelcome sexual attention or advances

### 🎭 Disruptive Behavior

-   Trolling, spamming, or deliberate inflammatory comments
-   Sustained disruption of discussions or events
-   Publishing others' private information without consent
-   Doxxing or stalking behavior

### 💼 Professional Misconduct

-   Misrepresenting your identity, experience, or affiliations
-   Plagiarism or claiming others' work as your own
-   Violating intellectual property rights
-   Spreading misinformation or false claims

### 🗣️ Communication Violations

-   Excessive profanity or inappropriate language
-   Off-topic discussions that derail productive conversations
-   Repeatedly ignoring community guidelines
-   Bad faith arguments or discussions

## 📢 Reporting Incidents

If you encounter any violations of this Code of Conduct or witness inappropriate behavior, we encourage you to report it promptly.

### 🔒 How to Report

**Email**: [Confidential reporting coming soon]

**GitHub Issues**: For non-sensitive matters, you can [open an issue](https://github.com/ifsvivek/VideoFetch/issues/new?template=custom.md)

**Direct Contact**: Reach out to project maintainers directly

### 📋 What to Include in Your Report

-   **📅 When**: Date and time of the incident
-   **📍 Where**: Location (GitHub issue, discussion, etc.)
-   **👥 Who**: Individuals involved (you can report anonymously)
-   **📝 What**: Description of what happened
-   **📎 Evidence**: Screenshots, links, or other documentation

### 🛡️ Our Commitment

-   **🔒 Confidentiality**: All reports will be treated with strict confidentiality
-   **⚡ Timely Response**: We will respond to reports promptly
-   **🔍 Fair Investigation**: All reports will be reviewed thoroughly and fairly
-   **⚖️ Appropriate Action**: We will take suitable action to address violations

---

## ⚖️ Consequences

Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, consequences may include:

### 📝 First Violation - Warning

-   **Action**: Private warning with explanation
-   **Goal**: Education and awareness
-   **Follow-up**: Monitoring for improvement

### 🚨 Second Violation - Temporary Restriction

-   **Action**: Temporary suspension from project participation
-   **Duration**: 1-30 days depending on severity
-   **Requirements**: Acknowledgment of behavior and commitment to change

### 🚫 Severe/Repeated Violations - Permanent Ban

-   **Action**: Permanent exclusion from the project
-   **Scope**: All project communication channels and repositories
-   **Review**: Possibility of appeal after 12 months

### 🚨 Immediate Action for Serious Violations

For severe violations (harassment, threats, doxxing), immediate permanent exclusion may be applied without prior warnings.

## 🌐 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community interactions, including:

### 💻 Online Spaces

-   GitHub repositories, issues, and pull requests
-   Project discussions and comments
-   Social media interactions related to the project
-   Email communications

### 🤝 Events and Meetings

-   Virtual meetings and conferences
-   In-person gatherings or meetups
-   Project-related presentations or talks

### 🔗 External Representation

-   Speaking on behalf of the project
-   Representing the project at external events
-   Social media activity that mentions the project

---

## 🔄 Enforcement Guidelines

Our community leaders will follow these guidelines when determining consequences:

### 1. 📊 Assessment Criteria

-   **Impact**: How the behavior affected the community
-   **Intent**: Whether the behavior was deliberate or accidental
-   **History**: Past behavior and any previous violations
-   **Response**: The individual's response when addressed

### 2. 🎯 Proportional Response

-   Consequences will be proportional to the violation
-   First-time minor violations may result in education rather than punishment
-   Serious violations will be met with immediate action

### 3. 📈 Focus on Improvement

-   Priority on helping individuals learn and grow
-   Support for positive behavioral change
-   Recognition of genuine efforts to improve
